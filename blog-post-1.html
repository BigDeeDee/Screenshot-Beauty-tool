如何让截图更好看？圆角与阴影的实用技巧

✍️ 截图美化团队 📅 2026年6月1日 ⏱️ 阅读时间：4分钟
🖼️✨

你有没有发现，同样是截图，有些人发的图看起来就很高级，而你的截图总感觉少了点什么？其实，秘密往往藏在两个小小的细节里——圆角和阴影。

今天，我们就来聊聊这两个不起眼的设计元素，如何让你的截图在一秒钟内从"普通"变得"精致"。

为什么圆角让截图更柔和？

默认情况下，所有截图都是尖锐的直角边缘。虽然这很精确，但也给人一种生硬、未加工的感觉。相比之下，圆角能够：

  • 降低视觉冲击：人眼处理圆角比直角更轻松，圆角引导视线更自然地流动。
  • 传达友好感：圆角在心理学上与安全、温和、亲近联系在一起，这也是为什么很多现代 UI 设计大量使用圆角。
  • 统一视觉风格：如果你把截图放在文章或社交媒体帖子中，圆角截图能更好地融入整体设计，不会显得突兀。

感觉生硬、未加工
✅ 有圆角
看起来柔和、精致

阴影：给截图增加层次感

扁平化的截图有时候会让人觉得"贴"在页面上，缺乏立体感。适当地添加阴影可以：

  • 增加深度感：阴影让截图看起来像是浮在背景之上，更有层次。
  • 突出截图的边界：当截图内容与背景颜色相近时，阴影可以清晰地勾勒出截图的轮廓。
  • 提升专业度：恰到好处的阴影是设计师常用的手法，能让你的截图瞬间拥有"专业感"。
💡 小贴士：阴影不宜过重。一般来说，透明度控制在15%-30%之间，模糊值在12-24px左右，效果最自然。过重的阴影反而会显得廉价。

圆角和阴影的最佳搭配

那么，什么样的参数组合能让截图最好看呢？根据我们的经验，这里有一些推荐的搭配：

🟢 清新简约风

  • 圆角：16-20px
  • 阴影模糊：12-16px
  • 阴影透明度：15-20%
  • 适合场景：博客文章、产品文档

🔵 精致卡片风

  • 圆角：20-28px
  • 阴影模糊：20-24px
  • 阴影透明度：20-25%
  • 适合场景：社交媒体分享、封面图

🟣 大胆突出风

  • 圆角：12-16px
  • 阴影模糊：24-32px
  • 阴影透明度：25-30%
  • 适合场景：产品展示、落地页

Have you ever noticed how some people's screenshots look incredibly polished while yours always seem to be missing something? The secret often lies in two tiny details—rounded corners and shadows.

Today, let's explore how these two understated design elements can transform your screenshots from "plain" to "polished" in seconds.

Why Rounded Corners Make Screenshots Softer?

By default, all screenshots have sharp, right‑angled edges. While precise, they can feel rigid and unrefined. In contrast, rounded corners:

  • Reduce visual tension: The human eye processes rounded corners more easily than sharp ones, allowing the gaze to flow more naturally.
  • Convey friendliness: Psychologically, rounded corners are associated with safety, warmth, and approachability—which is why modern UI design uses them extensively.
  • Unify visual style: When you place a screenshot inside an article or social media post, rounded corners help it blend seamlessly into the overall design rather than sticking out awkwardly.

Shadows: Adding Depth to Screenshots

Flat screenshots can sometimes feel "stuck" to the page, lacking dimension. A well‑placed shadow can:

  • Create depth: Shadows make screenshots appear to float above the background, adding visual hierarchy.
  • Define boundaries: When the screenshot content blends with the background color, a shadow clearly outlines its edges.
  • Boost professionalism: A tasteful shadow is a designer's trick that instantly gives your screenshots a "pro" look.
💡 Pro Tip: Less is more with shadows. Aim for 15%–30% opacity and 12–24px blur for the most natural effect. Heavy shadows tend to look cheap.

Best Combinations of Radius and Shadow

So what parameter combinations produce the best‑looking screenshots? Based on our experience, here are some recommended pairings:

🟢 Clean & Minimal

  • Corner radius: 16–20px
  • Shadow blur: 12–16px
  • Shadow opacity: 15–20%
  • Best for: Blog posts, product documentation

🔵 Elegant Card Style

  • Corner radius: 20–28px
  • Shadow blur: 20–24px
  • Shadow opacity: 20–25%
  • Best for: Social media sharing, cover images

🟣 Bold & Eye‑Catching

  • Corner radius: 12–16px
  • Shadow blur: 24–32px
  • Shadow opacity: 25–30%
  • Best for: Product showcases, landing pages
