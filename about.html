我们的故事

截图美化工具诞生于一个简单的需求：写公众号推文时，总觉得直接贴截图太生硬。加个圆角、添层阴影，观感立刻提升。但每次打开设计软件又太麻烦——于是就有了这个10秒出图的在线工具。

我们的理念很简单：好用的工具应该零门槛。不需要安装，不需要注册，打开浏览器就能用。所有处理在你自己的设备上完成，图片不会离开你的电脑。

Our Story

Screenshot Beautifier was born from a simple frustration: plain screenshots always looked too harsh in blog posts. Add rounded corners and a subtle shadow, and they instantly look polished. But opening design software every time was a hassle — so we built this 10-second online tool.

Our philosophy is simple: great tools should have zero barriers. No installation, no sign-up. Just open your browser and go. All processing happens on your device — your images never leave your computer.
